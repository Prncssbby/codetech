"http://localhost/phpmyadmin/"

To view the server folder
type this to your destination folder:
/var/www/html/codetech/

"Codetech" folder contains all the information including file,
images, scripts and your saved files.

To view your upload or saved files.
it is on the "/var/www/html/codetech/views/uploads/"

"/var/www/html/codetech/views/uploads/trash"

"/var/www/html/codetech/views/uploads/Retrieve"
